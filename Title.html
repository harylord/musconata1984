Vision Statement: To provide the best world class agriculture innovation to farmers
.
Mission: To Educate and provide Innovative services to Farmers and Agricultural organizations
.
Goals and Objectives of Agrinovate-Ghana
    To find innovative opportunities for members
    To partner with agencies for service opportunities
    To form cooperative bodies with members to manage their own entities
    To educate and train farmers on farming and marketing opportunities
    To advocate for the welfare of the members.
